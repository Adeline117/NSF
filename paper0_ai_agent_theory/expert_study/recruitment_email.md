## English Version

**Subject: Invitation to Expert Panel: Validating the First Formal Definition of AI Agents in Web3**

Dear [Expert Name],

We are writing to invite you to participate in a Delphi expert panel study as part of our NSF-funded research on AI agents operating on public blockchains.

### What is this study about?

We have developed the first formal definition and taxonomy of on-chain AI agents. The definition specifies four necessary conditions (on-chain actuation, environmental perception, autonomous decision-making, and adaptiveness) that distinguish AI agents from bots, scripts, smart contracts, and human users. The taxonomy classifies agents into 8 categories along three dimensions: Autonomy Level, Environment Type, and Decision Model.

Before publication, we need expert validation through a structured Delphi consensus process.

### Why you?

We are recruiting 12-15 experts with deep knowledge in one or more of the following areas:

- Artificial intelligence and multi-agent systems
- Blockchain technology and DeFi protocols
- Smart contract security and MEV
- Human-computer interaction and automation taxonomies
- AI agent frameworks (e.g., LangChain, AutoGPT, ELIZA)

Your published work in [specific area] makes you an ideal panelist for this study.

### What would you do?

This is a 2-round modified Delphi survey, conducted asynchronously via a structured questionnaire:

- **Round 1 (Open Exploration)**: Rate the necessity and clarity of each definitional condition, validate the 8 taxonomy categories, classify 10 real-world entities, and compare our framework against 5 existing taxonomies. (~30 minutes)

- **Round 2 (Convergence)**: Review the group's aggregated Round 1 results (medians, IQR) and re-rate items where consensus was not reached. (~20 minutes)

### 研究背景

我们提出了链上 AI Agent 的首个形式化定义与分类体系。该定义规定了四个必要条件（链上执行能力、环境感知、自主决策和适应性），用以区分 AI Agent 与机器人脚本、智能合约和人类用户。分类体系沿三个维度（自主性等级、环境类型、决策模型）将 Agent 分为8类。
