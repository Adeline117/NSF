## Purpose of the Study / 研究目的

You are being invited to participate in a research study that aims to validate a formal definition and taxonomy of AI agents operating on public blockchains (e.g., Ethereum). This is a Delphi expert consensus study that will inform a peer-reviewed publication.

您被邀请参与一项研究，旨在验证在公链（如以太坊）上运行的 AI Agent 的形式化定义和分类体系。这是一项德尔菲专家共识研究，将为同行评审论文提供支撑。

The study does NOT involve experimental interventions, medical procedures, or vulnerable populations. It is an expert elicitation study collecting professional opinions.

本研究不涉及实验干预、医疗程序或弱势群体。这是一项收集专业意见的专家咨询研究。

---

## What You Will Be Asked To Do / 参与内容

If you agree to participate, you will:

如果您同意参与，您将：

1. **Round 1 (~30 minutes)**: Complete a structured questionnaire that asks you to:
   - Rate the necessity and clarity of four definitional conditions (Likert 1-5)
   - Evaluate eight taxonomy categories for well-definedness and distinguishability
   - Classify 10 real-world blockchain entities into the taxonomy
   - Compare our framework against five existing agent taxonomies
   - Provide open-ended feedback and suggestions

   **第1轮（约30分钟）**：完成结构化问卷，包括：
   - 评价四个定义条件的必要性和清晰度（李克特1-5分）
   - 评估八个分类类别的定义清晰度和可区分度
   - 将10个真实区块链实体归入分类体系
   - 将我们的框架与五个现有 Agent 分类体系进行对比
   - 提供开放式反馈和建议

2. **Round 2 (~20 minutes)**: Review aggregated Round 1 results (group medians and IQR) and re-rate items where expert consensus was not reached.

   **第2轮（约20分钟）**：审阅第1轮汇总结果（小组中位数和四分位距），并对未达成共识的条目重新评分。

## Risks and Discomforts / 风险与不适

This study poses **minimal risk** to participants. The only foreseeable risks are:

本研究对参与者的风险为**最小风险**。可预见的风险仅包括：

- **Time investment**: Approximately 50 minutes total across two rounds.
- **Minor discomfort**: You may find some questions challenging or feel uncertain about classifications. You may skip any question you prefer not to answer.

- **时间投入**：两轮共计约50分钟。
- **轻微不适**：部分问题可能具有一定难度，或您可能对某些分类感到不确定。您可以跳过任何不愿回答的问题。

There are no physical, psychological, legal, social, or economic risks associated with this study.

本研究不存在身体、心理、法律、社会或经济方面的风险。

## Confidentiality / 保密性

Your individual responses will be kept **strictly confidential**:

您的个人回答将被**严格保密**：

- **During the study**: Individual responses will NOT be shared with other panelists. Only the research team will see individual data.
- **In publications**: Only aggregated statistics (medians, IQR, percentage agreement) will be reported. No individual responses or identifying information will appear in the paper.
- **Expert panel membership**: The list of participating experts may be published in the Acknowledgments section ONLY with your explicit consent (Option A above).
- **Data storage**: De-identified response data will be stored in encrypted form on institutional servers and retained for 5 years per university data retention policy. Only the PI and authorized research team members will have access.

- **研究期间**：个人回答不会与其他专家共享。仅研究团队可查看个人数据。
- **发表时**：仅报告汇总统计量（中位数、四分位距、一致性百分比）。论文中不会出现个人回答或身份识别信息。
- **专家组成员**：参与专家名单仅在您明确同意的情况下（上述选项A）才会在致谢部分中公布。
- **数据存储**：去识别化的回答数据将以加密形式存储在机构服务器上，按大学数据保留政策保留5年。仅首席研究员和授权研究团队成员有权访问。
